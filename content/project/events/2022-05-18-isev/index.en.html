In May, Dr Oksana Kehoe and Becky Davies (3rd Year PhD student) attended the International Society for Extracellular Vesicles Annual Meeting (ISEV2022) at the Centre des Congrès of Lyon, France.

The annual meeting offered an unparalleled opportunity to learn from, teach, and network with, leading and emerging experts in EV research.

This event featured the best in vesicle science, covering all aspects of fundamental, translational, and clinical research, disseminating cutting-edge developments in EV research. ISEV2022 brought together scientists from academic, clinics, and industry, who have a common goal of understanding EVs and applying this knowledge for societal and economic benefit.

Becky Davies is the winner of the ISEV2022 Scholarship and was selected for oral presentation with her abstract:

Rebecca Davies, Mairead Hyland, Claire Mennan, Karina Wright, Mark Platt, Oksana Kehoe ’The therapeutic effect of umbilical cord mesenchymal stromal cell extracellular vesicles in inflammatory arthritis.
