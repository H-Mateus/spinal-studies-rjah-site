The effect of different doses of ionizing radiation on the functional activity and mechanisms of the activation of the lymphoid cells from the spleen and thymus of rats exposed to a stimulating signal

M V Ruchko, L I Ostapchenko, Oksana Kehoe, N E Kucherenko
March 1994 publication

Abstract

The whole-body X-irradiation of rats with doses of 0.5 and 1 Gy has been shown to change the rosette forming ability of cells of immune organs and to decrease the stimulating effect of mitogenes (Con A, PHA and LPS) on Ca2+ phospholipidproteinkinase activity.
